01 Executive Summary

This brief reports the most frequently recurring build defects found at first inspection (Cycle 1) across {{ cohort_units }} of {{ total_4bed_units }} four-bed units. C1 is the as-built verdict — before any de-snagging — and is therefore the purest signal of build quality on hand-over from the trades. Items are ranked by units affected (the share of inspected units carrying that defect), not by raw count: a defect appearing in many units points to a systemic process problem, whereas a high raw count in a single unit does not. The four bedrooms are reported as one group — a defect found in three of a unit’s four bedrooms counts as one unit affected, though every occurrence is still tallied under “Logged”.
